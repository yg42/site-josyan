Welcome to **Les Jardins de Josyan** — a 70 m² house spread over two floors, in
Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, between Lyon and Saint-Étienne. A warm and well-equipped home,
with **breathtaking views of the Pilat massif and the Mont-Blanc range**,
facing due south.

## The accommodation

The house is arranged over two floors with three sleeping areas:

- 2 bedrooms with a **double bed**
- 1 bedroom with a **sofa bed**

The **kitchen is fully equipped**: oven, fridge, induction hob,
coffee maker, kettle, washing machine. The bathroom has an Italian-style walk-in shower.
TV and Wi-Fi included.

## Location

Saint-Martin-la-Plaine is an ideally situated village, halfway between Lyon (40 min)
and Saint-Étienne (20 min), a stone's throw from the Parc Naturel Régional du Pilat.

From the house, on foot:
- **3 min** — bakery, fromagerie, Vival, butcher
- **5 min** — start of marked hiking trails
- **15 min** — Espace Zoologique de Saint-Martin-la-Plaine

By car:
- **10 min** — Rive-de-Gier TER station (Lyon in 30 min, Saint-Étienne in 15 min)
- **15 min** — Sainte-Croix-en-Jarez, the Pilat
