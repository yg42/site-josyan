A **70 m² south-facing house** with views of the Pilat massif and the Mont-Blanc range, in
**Saint-Martin-la-Plaine** — 20 min from Saint-Étienne, 40 min from Lyon, and a stone's
throw from the Parc Naturel Régional du Pilat.

Perfect for a family stay, a hiking weekend, or a getaway between two of the region's great cities.

## The accommodation

- 2 bedrooms with a **double bed** + 1 bedroom with a **sofa bed** (up to 5 guests)
- Fully equipped kitchen: oven, induction hob, fridge, coffee maker, washing machine
- Bathroom with walk-in shower
- South-facing terrace, TV, Wi-Fi

## Nearby

**On foot** from the house:
- **3 min** — bakery, fromagerie, Vival convenience store, butcher
- **5 min** — start of marked hiking trails
- **15 min** — Espace Zoologique de Saint-Martin-la-Plaine

**By car**:
- **5 min** — Génilac indoor swimming pool
- **10 min** — Rive-de-Gier TER station (Lyon in 30 min, Saint-Étienne in 15 min)
- **20 min** — Sainte-Croix-en-Jarez, gastronomic restaurants
- **25 min** — Geoffroy-Guichard stadium (Saint-Étienne)
- **25 min** — tree climbing (Saint-Jean-Bonnefonds)
- **42 min** — Saint-Galmier (Badoit Springs)
- **37 min** — Lyon (Place Bellecour)
- **29 min** — Vienne
