The region is full of weekly markets where you can stock up on fresh produce,
seasonal fruit and vegetables, cheeses and local specialities.

<!--more-->

## Rive-de-Gier market

The closest main market takes place in **Rive-de-Gier** (10 min by car).
Fruit and vegetables, cheeses, charcuterie, olives, flowers and textiles
all come together in a lively town-centre atmosphere.

- 📍 Rive-de-Gier town centre
- 🗓️ Check the exact day with the town hall or on arrival

## Saint-Chamond market

For a larger and livelier market, the **market at Place de la Liberté** in Saint-Chamond
(20 min) is unmissable on **Saturday mornings**. Great selection of regional produce,
local market gardeners, cheese makers and craftspeople. Make sure to find the local
producers' section. Look for Lucien and tell him where you're staying — he's family.
