The **Vival shop** is the village's local convenience store, ideal for everyday
purchases without taking the car. Bread, fresh produce, drinks,
groceries — everything you need for a quick top-up or to complete your shopping.

<!--more-->

## What you'll find

General grocery, fresh produce (dairy, fruit and vegetables), drinks,
press, and everyday essentials. Opening hours are convenient, with Sunday morning
opening — handy for bread or milk if you've run out.

## Practical info

- 📍 **Vival** — saint-martin-la-plaine village centre
- 🚶 Within walking distance from the flat
- 🕐 Open 7 days a week, including Sunday mornings
