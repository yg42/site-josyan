Probably the best butcher in the area, **Boucherie Bayle** is **15 minutes by car**
from your accommodation. For a fine cut of beef, a leg of lamb for Sunday or
homemade charcuterie, this is the address to remember.

<!--more-->

## Products

Boucherie Bayle is renowned for the quality of its meat selection:
**Charolais beef**, **veal**, **lamb**, **pork** and **poultry** — everything is carefully
sourced from local producers. The homemade charcuterie (saucissons, pâtés, rillettes)
is also well worth the trip.

## Practical info

- 🚗 15 min from saint-martin-la-plaine
- 🔗 [boucherie-bayle.com](https://www.boucherie-bayle.com/)
