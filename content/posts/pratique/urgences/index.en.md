In the event of a medical or other emergency, here are the essential numbers and addresses
to have to hand during your stay.

<!--more-->

## Emergency numbers

| Service | Number |
|---|---|
| SAMU (medical emergencies) | **15** |
| Police / Gendarmerie | **17** |
| Fire brigade | **18** |
| Single European emergency number | **112** |
| SOS Médecins | **3600** |
| On-call doctor (nights/weekends) | **15** (SAMU will redirect) |

## Nearest hospital

**Centre Hospitalier de Rive-de-Gier** (Hôpital Léon Pasty)
Located approximately 10 min by car.

- 📍 Rue du Maréchal Foch — 42800 Rive-de-Gier
- ☎️ 04 77 75 20 20

## Nearby pharmacies

Several pharmacies in **Rive-de-Gier** (10 min) and in **Saint-Martin-la-Plaine** (village).
Check opening hours and on-call duty at:

- 🔗 [On-call pharmacies — Loire](https://www.ordre.pharmacien.fr/je-cherche-une-pharmacie)

## GP / General practitioner

Medical practices are available in rive-de-gier. For non-life-threatening emergencies,
you can also use:

- **Doctolib** to find an available doctor quickly: [doctolib.fr](https://www.doctolib.fr)
- **15 (SAMU)** for any urgent medical guidance

## Dentist / Optician

Rive-de-Gier has several dentists and opticians.
Quick search via [doctolib.fr](https://www.doctolib.fr) or Pages Jaunes.
