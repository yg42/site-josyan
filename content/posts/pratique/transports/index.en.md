The flat is ideally located between Lyon and Saint-Étienne, both easily reachable by car
or by train from the TER station at **Rive-de-Gier**, 10 minutes away.

<!--more-->

## Rive-de-Gier TER station

Rive-de-Gier station is on the **Lyon — Saint-Étienne** line (TER Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes).

- 🚗 **From the flat**: 10 min by car, free parking near the station
- 🚂 **To Saint-Étienne Châteaucreux**: approximately 15 min journey
- 🚂 **To Lyon Part-Dieu**: approximately 35 to 40 min journey
- 🕐 Frequent trains on weekdays, fewer at weekends — check live timetables

## To Lyon

Lyon is accessible:
- By **TER** from rive-de-gier (~40 min to Part-Dieu)
- By **car** via the A47 then A7 (~40–50 min depending on traffic)

From Lyon Part-Dieu, direct access to the TCL network (metro, tram) to reach Vieux-Lyon,
the Presqu'île, Croix-Rousse or the Confluences district.

## To Saint-Étienne

Saint-Étienne is accessible:
- By **TER** from rive-de-gier (~15 min)
- By **car** via the A47 (~20 min)

From Saint-Étienne Châteaucreux, tram lines T1 and T3 connect to the city centre, the Cité du Design
and the Geoffroy-Guichard stadium.

## Other connections

- **Bus**: STAS and departmental coach services connect Saint-Martin-la-Plaine and Rive-de-Gier
- **Carpooling**: The A47 is a busy route — BlaBlaCar available for Lyon and Saint-Étienne
