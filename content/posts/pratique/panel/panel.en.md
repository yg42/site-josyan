**Boucherie Panel** is **5 minutes' walk** from the flat, right in the heart of
Saint-Martin-la-Plaine. It's the village butcher — handy for a quick purchase
without taking the car.

## Products

Fresh meats, poultry and daily charcuterie: Boucherie Panel covers your everyday
needs with friendly service and quality products.
Perfect for an impromptu barbecue or chops for the evening.
