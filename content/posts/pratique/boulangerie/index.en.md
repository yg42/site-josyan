For your daily bread, morning croissants and pastries, a bakery is
within walking distance from the flat, right in the heart of the village.

<!--more-->

## In the village

Saint-Martin-la-Plaine has a bakery in the village centre,
just a few minutes' walk from the flat.

Traditional bread, baguettes, speciality loaves, croissants and fresh pastries
every morning.
