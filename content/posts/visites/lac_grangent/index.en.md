The Lac de Grangent is a reservoir on the Loire, nestled in a landscape of wooded gorges.
It is **the unmissable swimming and outdoor destination** in the area, just **35 minutes** from
Saint-Martin-la-Plaine.

<!--more-->

## The lake and its scenery

Formed by the EDF dam at Grangent, the lake stretches for several kilometres through a setting
of cliffs and pine-covered hills. At the centre of the lake stands the **château de Grangent**,
a picturesque island with the ruins of a medieval castle and a chapel — accessible by boat.

## Activities

- 🏊 **Swimming**: several equipped beaches, including the beach at Saint-Victor-sur-Loire (listed among the most beautiful villages in France)
- 🚣 **Kayak and canoe**: hire available on site in season
- 🚴 **Cycling**: quiet paths and roads around the lake
- 🚶 **Hiking**: discovery trails through the Loire gorges
- ⛵ **Boat trips**: commented outings on the lake in summer (departing from Saint-Victor-sur-Loire)

## Saint-Victor-sur-Loire

This village, listed among the **Plus Beaux Villages de France**, overlooks the lake from its
heights. Medieval lanes, exceptional viewpoint, restaurants with terraces — ideal for a half-day.

## Practical info

- 📍 **Main access**: Saint-Victor-sur-Loire (commune of Saint-Étienne)
- 🚗 **Distance**: ~35 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine (A47 then Saint-Étienne ring road)
- 🗓️ **Best period**: May to September (swimming and water activities)
