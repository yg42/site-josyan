**20 minutes** from the apartment, the **Musée des Verts** traces the epic story of AS
Saint-Étienne, one of the most decorated and legendary clubs in French football.
Even without being a supporter, the history of this club is the story of an entire city.

<!--more-->

## A legend of French football

ASSE has won **10 French league titles** and **6 French Cups**.
At the height of their glory in the 1970s, the club reached the final of the European
Cup of Champions in 1976, losing narrowly to Bayern Munich.
That match remains etched in the collective memory.

## What you discover at the museum

The museum traces the entire history of the club through:

- **Historical kits** from every era
- **Cups and trophies** won over the decades
- **Photos and archive footage** of great matches and legendary players
- Interactive terminals and kids' spaces
- The gallery of **iconic players**: Salif Keïta, Dominique Rocheteau, Michel Platini…
