**20 minutes** from the apartment, the **MAMC+** (Musée d'Art Moderne et Contemporain de
Saint-Étienne) holds one of the finest modern art collections in France.
A nationally ranked museum, often underestimated, that is truly worth the visit.

<!--more-->

## An exceptional collection

The MAMC+ houses more than **20,000 works** covering art from the 20th century to the present:
Cubism, Surrealism, Minimalism, Conceptual art, and international contemporary art.

Among the featured artists: Fernand Léger, Le Corbusier, Jean Dubuffet, Andy Warhol,
Christian Boltanski, and a strong collection of artists from the region and the École de
Nice.

## Temporary exhibitions

The museum regularly refreshes its **temporary exhibitions**, often thematic
and remarkably well staged. Check the programme before your visit — some
exhibitions draw visitors from across France.

## The building

The museum is housed in a sleek, light-filled modern building, designed to showcase
the works. It stands at the entrance to the Cité du Design, making it easy to combine
both visits in a single day.

## Practical info

- 📍 **MAMC+** — Rue Fernand Léger, Saint-Étienne
- 🚗 20 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
- 💶 Full price: ~€8 — **free on the 1st Sunday of the month**
- 🔗 [mamc.saint-etienne.fr](https://mamc.saint-etienne.fr/fr)
