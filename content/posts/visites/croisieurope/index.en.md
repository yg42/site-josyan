**CroisiEurope** offers festive weekend cruises by boat on the Rhône between Lyon and Vienne,
themed around the great musical decades (60s, 70s, 80s, disco…).

<!--more-->

## Options

- **Festive weekend** — 2 days / 1 night on board, full board, entertainment and themed evenings
- **Long cruises** — itineraries to Provence, Ardèche or the Camargue (7 to 8 days)

## Practical info

- 📍 Departure: quai Claude Bernard, Lyon (at the foot of the university district)
- 🚗 **Distance**: 40 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
- 🔗 [croisieurope.com](https://www.croisieurope.com/)
