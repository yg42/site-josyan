**Fromagerie Grange** is right in the heart of Saint-Martin-la-Plaine,
just a few minutes' walk from the apartment. A must-visit address to put together
a cheeseboard or leave with some excellent local cheeses.

<!--more-->

## The products

The cheese shop offers a wide selection of aged, fresh and regional cheeses:
**Saint-Marcellin**, **Reblochon**, **goat's cheeses from the Jarez**, **tome de Savoie**,
and many other artisan dairy products.

Alongside the cheeses, you will often find **yoghurts**, **farmhouse butter**,
**fresh eggs** and a few fine grocery items to round off your shopping.

## Perfect for a cheeseboard

Whether for a spontaneous aperitif, a picnic in the Pilat or simply the pleasure of
good cheese, Fromagerie Grange is the best option for several kilometres around.
