Saint-Étienne, **20 minutes by car** (or 15 min by TER from Rive-de-Gier), is a city
undergoing transformation, combining industrial heritage with cultural dynamism.

<!--more-->

## Cité du Design

Housed in the former arms factory, the Cité du Design is a must-see venue
dedicated to design in all its forms. Permanent and temporary exhibitions, shop,
cafeteria in a spectacular building.

- 📍 3 rue Javelin Pagnon — Saint-Étienne
- 🔗 [citedudesign.com](https://www.citedudesign.com)

## Stade Geoffroy-Guichard — an ASSE match

Football fans won't want to miss a match of **AS Saint-Étienne**,
one of France's most decorated clubs. The atmosphere at the Chaudron is worth the trip.

- 📍 Rue Paul et Pierre Guichard — Saint-Étienne
- 🎟️ Tickets: [asse.fr](https://www.asse.fr)

## Eating out and strolling around

The **Cours Fauriel**, **rue Général Foy** and the area around **Place du Peuple**
are home to numerous cafés, brasseries and restaurants. The market at **Place Carnot**
is unmissable on Saturday mornings.

## Practical info

- 🚂 **By TER**: depart from Rive-de-Gier, ~15 min to Saint-Étienne Châteaucreux
