Just **15 minutes** from the apartment, the **Ferme des Chartreux** is set at the heart
of the medieval village of Sainte-Croix-en-Jarez — itself nestled within a 13th-century
former charterhouse. An exceptional setting to buy cheese straight from the farm.

<!--more-->

## The cheeses

The dairy produces its cheeses from the milk of their goats and cows:
**tommes**, **fresh cheeses**, **goat's cheese logs**, **yoghurts**, **aged cheeses**…
Everything is home-made, in the tradition of the cheese farms of the Jarez.

## Combine the visit with a tour of the village

Sainte-Croix-en-Jarez deserves a visit in its own right — it is one of the most
unusual villages in France, where families still live in the cells and cloisters of a
medieval charterhouse. Allow an hour to wander freely through the village,
then finish with a stop at the farm.

## Nearby walks

Several marked trails set off from the village, through the hills of the Jarez.
The combination of a walk, cheese and a picnic is perfect for a relaxed day out.
