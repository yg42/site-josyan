The Parc Naturel Régional du Pilat stretches just a short distance from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine.
Its ridges, beech forests and panoramic views over the Alps make it **the ultimate nature
playground** for all levels.

<!--more-->

## The summits

The Pilat reaches its highest point at the **Crêt de la Perdrix** (1,432 m), accessible
from several starting points. On a clear day, the view stretches from the Alps to the Massif Central.

The most iconic viewpoints:
- **Crêt de la Perdrix** — highest point of the massif (1,432 m)
- **Crêt de l'Œillon** — antenna and 360° panorama (1,370 m)
- **Pic des Trois Dents** — distinctive silhouette visible from the plain

## Hikes and activities

- 🥾 More than **600 km of marked trails** for all levels
- 🚴 **Mountain biking and cycling**: mountain passes and forest tracks
- 🌿 **Foraging**: blueberries, mushrooms depending on the season
- ❄️ **Snowshoeing** in winter on the high plateaus

## From Saint-Martin-la-Plaine

The apartment is located on the edge of the Pilat: the **Crêt Féchet** hike (starting from
the village) is already a fine introduction. For longer outings, head to:

- **Pélussin** (Rhône valley side, Condrieu vineyards) — ~30 min
- **Bourg-Argental** (heart of the park) — ~35 min
- **Planfoy** (quick access to the ridges from Saint-Étienne) — ~30 min

## Practical info

- 🚗 **Access from the village**: 15 to 30 min depending on the area
- 🏠 **Maison du Parc**: Pélussin — exhibitions, hiking info, IGN maps
- 🔗 [parc-naturel-pilat.fr](https://www.parc-naturel-pilat.fr)
