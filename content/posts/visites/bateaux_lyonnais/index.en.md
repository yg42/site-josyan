**Les Bateaux Lyonnais** offer cruises on the Saône departing from the heart of Lyon,
between the Île Barbe and the Confluence.

<!--more-->

## Options

- **Commented sightseeing cruise** — discover Lyon's quays and bridges from the water
- **Lunch cruise** — 2h30, departure 12:30pm
- **Dinner cruise** — 2h30, departure 8:30pm
- **Private hire** — birthdays, weddings, corporate events

## Practical info

- 📍 2 quai des Célestins, Lyon 2nd (foot of the Palais de Justice footbridge)
- 🚗 **Distance**: 40 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
- 🔗 [lesbateauxlyonnais.com](https://www.lesbateauxlyonnais.com/)
