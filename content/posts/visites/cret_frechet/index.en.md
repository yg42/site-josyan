The Crêt Féchet circuit is **the must-do hike** starting from
Saint-Martin-la-Plaine. Marked in white and yellow as trail no. 3, it climbs to
a summit at **630 metres**, offering an exceptional panorama over the entire region.

## The route

Along the way, you pass through meadows, cherry orchards and forests typical of the
Jarez hills. The path runs alongside the Espace Zoologique at certain points — a chance to spot
a few residents from outside the enclosures!

At the summit, the viewpoint reveals a breathtaking view of:

- The **Pilat massif** and its wooded ridges
- The villages of **Saint-Martin-la-Plaine** and **Saint-Joseph** below
- The **Monts du Lyonnais** to the north
- On a clear day, the **Alps** on the horizon

## Practical info

- 🟡 **Waymarking**: white and yellow, trail no. 3
- ⛰️ **Highest point**: Crêt Féchet, 630 m altitude
- 🚶 **Starting point**: Village centre of Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Suitable for**: Families, sturdy footwear recommended
- ⏱️ **Estimated duration**: approx. 2h30 to 3h depending on pace

## Tips

Set off in the morning to make the most of the light on the Pilat, and on a clear day
to have a chance of seeing the Alps from the summit.
