Just **1h15** from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, **Canotiers Boat'n Bike** offer original
river outings on the Rhône, departing from Sainte-Colombe (across from Vienne).

<!--more-->

## The boat

A **larch wood catamaran** powered by solar energy, 60 passengers, with a deck and bar on board.

## Options

- **Boat & Bike** — a day combining a Rhône cruise and return by bike on the ViaRhôna or the Dolce Via (bikes carried on board for free)
- **Apéro cruise** — a Rhône trip with drinks
- **Wine cruise** — tasting of Côtes-du-Rhône wines on board
- **Dinner cruise**
- **Private hire** from 400 €


## Practical info

- 📍 Sainte-Colombe (69560) — Ardèche bank of the Rhône, accessible on foot from Vienne via the bridge
- 🚗 **Distance**: approx. 1h15 from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
