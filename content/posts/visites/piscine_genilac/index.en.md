The **Centre Nautique Intercommunal André Chazalon** in Génilac is **5 minutes by car**
from the apartment — the nearest indoor pool, open year-round.

<!--more-->

## Facilities

- **25 m main pool** — 6 lanes, for swimming and aquagym
- **150 m² leisure pool** — hydromassage jets and massage bench
- **40 m water slide** (pentagliss) with splash pool
- **40 m² paddling pool** — perfect for toddlers
- **2 saunas**
- **Outdoor splashpad** and sun terrace

## Activities

In addition to free swimming, the centre offers classes and supervised sessions:
children's and adult swimming, baby swimming, prenatal swimming, aquagym, aquafitness,
and special aquaphobia sessions.
