Just **25 minutes** from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, **France Aventures Saint-Étienne** offers
tree climbing courses for all ages, open from February to November.

<!--more-->

## The courses

- **Forêt des Garagnas** — ground-level activities, from age 2
- **Parcours Ouistiti** — from 1 m tall, continuous safety net
- **Intermediate courses** — from 1.10 m tall
- **Expert courses** — from 1.40 m tall, with long-range zip lines
- **Night tree climbing** — from age 10 (32 €)
