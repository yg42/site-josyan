The **Ferme des Ruminettes** is an educational farm and artisan cheese dairy a few
minutes from the apartment, ideal for a family outing with young children.
Come to get close to the animals, taste fresh cheeses and head home with your arms
full of farm produce.

<!--more-->

## The animals

The farm is home to goats, sheep, donkeys, rabbits and poultry. Children can
feed them and approach them in the enclosures. A simple and authentic moment, far
from the hustle and bustle.

## The cheese dairy

The cheeses are made on site from the milk of the farm's own goats:
**fresh cheeses**, **logs**, **crottins**, **aged cheeses**… The shop also offers
yoghurts, local honey and other artisan products.

## Visits and activities

**Guided farm tours** are offered regularly, especially during school holidays.
Ideal for explaining to children where their food comes from
and how cheese is made.
