La Maison des Forgerons, also known as **La Mourine**, is a museum at the heart of
Saint-Martin-la-Plaine that traces the industrial and craft history of ironworking in
the Jarez region. Free entry — not to be missed if you are curious about local history.

<!--more-->

## Heritage rooted in local history

The village of Saint-Martin-la-Plaine was long a major centre for coal mining
and metalworking. From the Middle Ages onward, forges and foundries set the rhythm of local life.
La Maison des Forgerons honours this past by reconstructing a period workshop
where tools, machines and testimonies come together.

The museum trail weaves past and present closely together, helping visitors understand
how this expertise shaped the identity of the village and the entire Gier valley.
