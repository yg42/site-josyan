Just **45 minutes** from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, the **Espace Eaux Vives du Pilat Rhodanien**
offers water activities on an artificial river at the foot of the Rhône dam,
and on calm water — for all levels from age 6.

<!--more-->

## Whitewater (from age 14)

- Free or guided rafting
- Kayak / inflatable canoe, free use
- Whitewater swimming (hydrospeed), canoraft.

## Calm water (from age 6)

- Canoe-kayak
- Stand-up paddleboarding

Equipment provided (life jacket, paddle). Swimming ability required.

## On site

- Restaurant **Le Bouillon — Brasserie des Eaux-Vives**
- Campsite, chalets and gîtes (Maison de la Lône, open year-round)
- **ViaRhôna** access (cycle path), Accueil Vélo label
- Volleyball, pétanque, walks

## Practical info

- 📍 Avenue du Rhône, Saint-Pierre-de-Bœuf (42520)
- 🚗 **Distance**: approx. 45 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
- 🕐 9am – 6pm in season (April – September) — by reservation out of season
- 🔗 [espaceeauxvives.com](https://www.espaceeauxvives.com/)
