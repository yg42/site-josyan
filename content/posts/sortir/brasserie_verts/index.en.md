Located above the ticket office of the Geoffroy-Guichard stadium, la brasserie des Verts
offers local Stéphanois cuisine in a setting unlike any other.

<!--more-->

## A unique setting

From the 450 m² dining room, an **enormous glass wall looks directly out onto the pitch**
of the stadium, the historic home of AS Saint-Étienne. The décor pays tribute to the great
moments of les Verts through archives, photos and iconic club memorabilia. The venue
is open to everyone, supporters and non-supporters alike.

## The cuisine

Generous French cuisine rooted in Stéphanois tradition: râpée stéphanoise,
grilled andouillette, tartare, beef fillet, homemade "Burger du stade" burger.
The menu changes regularly, and the house offers its own **Whisky Geoffroy Guichard**.
