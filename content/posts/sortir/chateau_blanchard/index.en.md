**1 Michelin star since 2017.** Just 30 minutes from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, Château Blanchard
offers an exceptional gastronomic experience in a Loire château setting, between Lyon
and Saint-Étienne.

<!--more-->

## The cuisine

Chef **Sylvain Roux** crafts a contemporary French gastronomic menu, drawing on seasonal
Loire Valley produce. The wine list, curated by sommelier Frédéric Roux, is remarkable.

The establishment also has a hotel for those wishing to extend their stay.

## The menus

- 💶 Inspiration Menu: **89 € – 112 €** (with or without wine pairings)
- 💶 Carte Blanche Menu: **129 € – 140 €**
- 💶 Children's menu: **35 €**
