**2 Michelin stars. 5 Gault & Millau toques. Relais & Châteaux.**
Just 35 minutes from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, La Pyramide is one of the most legendary
restaurants in France — founded in 1822, the birthplace of nouvelle cuisine française.

<!--more-->

## A legendary house

La Pyramide owes its legend to **Fernand Point** (1897–1955), the first chef in the world
to earn three Michelin stars. He trained an entire generation of great chefs: Paul Bocuse,
the Troisgros brothers, Alain Chapel… The boulevard where the restaurant stands today
bears his name.

Since 1989, **Patrick Henriroux** has upheld the standard of excellence with two Michelin
stars maintained uninterrupted since 1992. His team includes **Michaël Bouvier**,
Meilleur Ouvrier de France 2019.

## The three spaces

- 🍽️ **Gastronomic restaurant** — creative cuisine showcasing local terroir produce
- 🌿 **Bistrot L'Espace Ph3** — generous, plant-forward cuisine in a verdant setting
- 🍸 **Blue Pearl Lounge Bar** — intimate bar open in the evenings
