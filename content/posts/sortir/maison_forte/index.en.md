Just 15 minutes from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, **La Maison Forte** is the area's benchmark
gastronomic restaurant. Selected by the Michelin Guide, awarded **13/20 by Gault & Millau**
and the Young Talents Prize, it stands out as an unmissable address.

<!--more-->

## The cuisine

Chef **Mickaël Marcoux**, trained at La Bouitte (3 Michelin stars in Savoie), crafts
a French gastronomic menu rooted in local produce and short supply chains.
Multi-course menus that change with the seasons.
