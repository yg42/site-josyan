**Michelin Bib Gourmand 2025** — "Good cuisine at reasonable prices." La Table des Matrus
is Saint-Étienne's trendiest bistronomic restaurant: natural wines, homemade ferments,
produce from carefully selected small local growers.

<!--more-->

## The cuisine

Young chef **Matéo Ravel** offers a natural and vibrant French cuisine:
homemade sourdough bread, beer and kefir fermented on site, artisan lardo, fresh daily fish.
A short menu that changes regularly, with a carefully selected network of producers.

An address that will delight lovers of contemporary cuisine and natural wines.

- 🚗 ~20 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
