In the heart of the Parc Naturel Régional du Pilat, at **1,300 metres altitude**, the Auberge
de la Jasserie is an authentic mountain dining experience. Perfect after a hike or for a
Sunday family lunch in a stunning setting. Paragliding is also available.

<!--more-->

## The cuisine

Generous, homemade mountain cooking: artisan charcuterie, slow-cooked dishes,
Pilat morels, bilberry tart made from berries picked on the ridges. Everything is made
on site, in the tradition of country inns.

## The setting

La Jasserie is a traditional Pilat farmhouse inn, built from dry stone, nestled
in high-altitude meadows between fir and beech trees. In winter, snowshoes are welcome;
in summer, hiking trails start right from the door.
