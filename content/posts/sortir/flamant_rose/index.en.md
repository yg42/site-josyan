Le Flamant Rose is a semi-gastronomic restaurant located **within the commune itself** of
Saint-Martin-la-Plaine, in a truly original natural setting.

<!--more-->

## A unique setting

The restaurant is built on **stilts over a 600 m² basin** populated with fish and turtles.
The terrace offers an open view of the neighbouring zoological park.
Flexible rooms for family meals, seminars or receptions for up to 300 covers.
