Located in the **Château Morel**, a fully restored early 20th-century building,
L'Éclosion offers inventive gastronomic cuisine centred on organic produce,
just a few minutes from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine.

<!--more-->

## The chef and his cuisine

**Pierre Carducci**, a graduate of the Institut Paul Bocuse and former member of the brigades
of Paul Bocuse and Christian Tetedoie in Lyon, opened L'Éclosion at the age of 21. The son
of an organic market gardener, he places **vegetables at the heart of the plate** and sources
produce directly from the estate's kitchen garden and the family farm.

The menu is built around **tasting menus of 8 to 9 courses**, with bold flavour combinations
and a cellar of 300 references with a focus on organic and natural wines.
