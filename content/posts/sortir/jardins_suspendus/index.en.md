Just 5 minutes away by car, the Domaine des Jardins Suspendus offers creative bistronomic
cuisine in an elegant riverside setting on the banks of the Gier. Rated **4.5/5 on TripAdvisor**.

<!--more-->

## The cuisine

Chef Arnaud Save-Morby offers a colourful and creative cuisine with travel-inspired influences,
playing on seasonal produce and unexpected combinations. An affordable lunchtime menu,
and a more elaborate evening menu centred on land and sea.

## The setting

Terrace with a basin on the banks of the Gier, landscaped gardens, bright dining room.
The estate also hosts **dinner shows** and **wine workshops**
led by a Meilleur Ouvrier de France.

## Practical info

- 📍 41 rue Antoine Marrel — 42800 Rive-de-Gier
- 🚗 ~5 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
