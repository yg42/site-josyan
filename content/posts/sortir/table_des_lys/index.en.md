**À la Table des Lys** is a renowned gastronomic restaurant
in Saint-Étienne, 20 minutes from the apartment. An exceptional experience for
special occasions.

<!--more-->

## The cuisine

Chef **Marc Lecroisey** delivers contemporary French gastronomic cuisine — light
and creative, showcasing seasonal produce and regional growers.
The service matches the food: attentive, precise, without excessive formality.

**4.8/5 on TripAdvisor** — top restaurant in Saint-Étienne (over 1,300 reviews).

## Practical info

- 📍 58 rue Saint-Simon — 42000 Saint-Étienne
- 🚗 ~20 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
