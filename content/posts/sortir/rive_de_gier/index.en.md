Rive-de-Gier, **10 minutes by car**, is the closest town for an evening out,
finding a restaurant or doing some shopping. Here is what's on offer.

<!--more-->

## Restaurants

Rive-de-Gier has several restaurants concentrated mainly around the
**Cours Gambetta** and the **Place du 8 Mai**:

- Traditional French restaurants
- World cuisine (Chinese food, pizza, etc.)
- Brasseries and cafés

For takeaway or delivery pizzas, two addresses are listed on this site:
[Pizza Dany](/posts/pratique/pizza/dany/) and [Pizza Cosy](/posts/pratique/pizza/cosy/).

## Market

The **Rive-de-Gier market** takes place in the town centre. Fruit and vegetables, cheeses,
charcuterie, flowers — ideal for putting together a picnic or picking up fresh produce.

## Supermarkets

Several large supermarkets in Rive-de-Gier for weekly shopping:
- **Carrefour Market** — Cours Gambetta
- **Lidl** — retail park
- **Intermarché** — near the town centre

## Cinema

The **Cinéma Le Jacquard** in Rive-de-Gier offers a varied programme,
from mainstream films to art-house screenings.

- 📍 Rive-de-Gier (town centre)
- 🔗 Check the programme at [allocine.fr](https://www.allocine.fr)

## Practical info

- 🚗 **Distance**: 10 min from Saint-Martin-la-Plaine
- 🚂 **By train**: Rive-de-Gier station (Lyon — Saint-Étienne line)
