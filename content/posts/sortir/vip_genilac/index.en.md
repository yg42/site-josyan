**VIP — Votre Instant Plaisir** is a festive brasserie in Génilac, right next to exit 12
of the A47 motorway, between Saint-Étienne and Lyon. Restaurant, bar, cellar, drive-through
and event venue — all in one place.

<!--more-->

## The venue

VIP offers several spaces: a **restaurant dining room**, a bar, a **Village à Bulles**
(bubble seating area — book in advance) and a summer terrace. The **cellar stocks over
800 references** (beers, wines and spirits from Europe, Asia, the Americas and Oceania).

With more than **200 dates a year**, the venue regularly hosts DJ nights, concerts,
karaoke evenings, sports screenings and other events.

## The food

Revisited traditional cuisine using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, entirely homemade.
Specialities: **burgers awarded at the French Burger Championship**, beef tataki, tapas,
sharing boards, salads, pasta, ravioli, fish, meats and homemade desserts.
